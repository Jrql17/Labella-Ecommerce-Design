About Us

Labella is company that was created by Juliana Cabuay in her early years as a student, upon reaching numerous customers throughout the years she decided to transition from a Make-Up only business into a Resupplier for Local Start-Up Makeup Stores in the Philippines. After delving and expanding in that market she had also entered the market of Clothing Rebrands as well.

The Labella owner also has her own account in Instagram named "LabellaRebrandingPH" to also cater with other people through the use of Social Media, furthermore the owner is also self-employed and a hardworking student from Mapua University. With countless experience in the market and academically the quality of the products that she delivers for her customer is catered with care and excellence.

Labella is a stock corporation incorporated under the laws of the Philippines and registered with the Philippine Securities and Exchange Commission on May 29, 2013. The Company is primarily engaged in buying, selling, distributing, marketing, at wholesale and retail, of all kinds of goods, commodities, wares and merchandise of every kind and description and enter into all kinds of contracts for the export, import, purchase, acquisition, sale at wholesale or retail and other disposition for its own account as principal or in representative capacity as manufacturer representative, merchandise broker, indentor, commission merchant, factors or agents, upon consignment of all kinds of goods, wares, merchandise or products whether natural or artificial.
